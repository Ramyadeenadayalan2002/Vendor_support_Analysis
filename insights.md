# Insights from Vendor Support Data

## Key Findings

- Total Tickets: 30
- Negative Response Rate (NRR): 50%
- Average Resolution Time: ~19 mins

## Observations

- Catalog-related issues have the highest volume of tickets.
- Negative responses are more frequent in Catalog and Returns categories.
- Audit flagged cases are strongly associated with negative responses.
- Higher resolution time correlates with negative feedback.

## Conclusion
Improving resolution efficiency and strengthening SOP guidance for high-volume categories like Catalog can significantly reduce NRR and improve vendor experience.
